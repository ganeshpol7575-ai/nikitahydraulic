About Nikita Hydraulic

Who We Are

Nikita Hydraulic is a specialized manufacturer of custom rubber products developed according to customer samples, engineering drawings, blueprints and technical specifications. We focus on precision, durability and consistent industrial quality.

What We Manufacture

Rubber Bushes, O-Rings, Oil Seals, Rubber Gaskets, Bellows, Rubber Boots, Dampers, Rubber Pads and customized industrial rubber moulded components.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable custom rubber solutions that improve machine performance, reduce downtime and provide long service life.

Our Commitment

Every product passes through strict quality inspection before dispatch. Customer satisfaction and long-term relationships are our highest priority.
