ABOUT US

Leading Custom Rubber Manufacturer in Pune

Nikita Hydraulic manufactures premium quality custom rubber moulded components according to customer drawings, CAD files and physical samples.

We specialize in developing high-performance rubber components for industrial, automotive, hydraulic, electrical and engineering applications.

Every product is manufactured according to customer specifications instead of using standard ready-made designs. We ensure excellent dimensional accuracy, long service life and reliable performance.

Our experienced team works closely with customers to develop rubber components that perfectly match their technical requirements.
